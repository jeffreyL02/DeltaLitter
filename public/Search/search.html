Plastic Bottle

General Info

A plastic bottle is a bottle constructed from plastic, usually polyethelene. Plastic bottles are typically used to store liquids such as water, soft drinks, motor oil, cooking oil, medicine, shampoo, milk, and ink.

Recyclability

Plastic bottles are recyclable.

Reusability

You can reuse a plastic bottle to make a battery recycler. Wait for all of the water to dry, then place batteries inside of it. When the bottle reaches max capacity, recycle it! Search for Battery Recycling Centers..
